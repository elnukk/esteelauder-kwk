Day vs Night

Skin is biologically different from day to night. Every night, your skin repairs damage and re-energizes. It is also more prone to moisture loss and has a thinner, more fragile barrier. That’s why we recommend having a dedicated nighttime routine, made with ingredients that can deliver powerful renewing benefits overnight.

Beauty Sleep is Real

Skin renews itself at night, and this process peaks while you sleep. Getting enough quality, uninterrupted sleep each night is essential in waking up with beautiful, glowing skin and helping prevent visible signs of aging.

Night Science

Our Estée Lauder scientists are experts in night sleep science, with 51 patents and 36 publications. Our first-ever groundbreaking clinical study unveiled that sleep-deprived skin can experience a weaker barrier and dryness, loss of firmness, a more uneven skintone, and increased lines.
